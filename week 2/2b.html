Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham

Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham is a multi-disciplinary, research-intensive, private university in India. It is known for its focus on academic excellence, innovation, and humanitarian values. The university offers a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs across various fields including engineering, medicine, business, and arts.

Founded by Mata Amritanandamayi Devi, the institution emphasizes value-based education and global impact through research and collaborations.
