Formula 1 Racers & Cars

Max Verstappen

Max Verstappen drives for Red Bull Racing.

is known for incredible aerodynamics,
extreme speed on straights,
and dominance in modern Formula 1.

Lewis Hamilton

Lewis Hamilton races for Mercedes AMG.
The Mercedes W14/W15 cars are known
for engineering excellence,
hybrid power units,
and championship-winning performance.

Charles Leclerc

Charles Leclerc drives for Ferrari.
Ferrari cars like the SF-23

powerful engines,
