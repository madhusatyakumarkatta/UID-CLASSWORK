Project: Mano Mitra by BYTE MINDS

Mano Mitra is a web platform created for parents of neurodivergent children. The goal of the project is to provide clarity, emotional reassurance, and early guidance using technology.

The platform helps parents differentiate between typical and atypical child behaviour using observation-based inputs such as text, images, videos, and personalized questionnaires.

AI-assisted analysis converts real-life observations into understandable assessments, helping parents decide whether further professional consultation is needed.
